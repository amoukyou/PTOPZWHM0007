方法论Methodology: 新增买入力 = 净流入（非总AUM）x 葡股配置% x PSI重叠率。存量AUM对应的股票已在市场中，只有净流入（新钱）才产生额外买入压力。净流入 = ΔAUM - (AUM_prev x 市场表现贡献)。IMGA 2024年报披露的实际净销售额€34.7M与公式估算的€34.9M误差仅0.6%，验证了方法的准确性。 New Buying Power = Net Inflow (not total AUM) x PT Equity % x PSI Overlap. Existing AUM stocks are already in the market; only new inflows create additional buying pressure. Net Flow = ΔAUM - (AUM_prev x Market Return). IMGA's 2024 annual report disclosed actual net sales of EUR34.7M vs our estimate of EUR34.9M (0.6% error), validating the methodology.

估算 GV 投资者人数Estimated GV Investors

为什么用净流入而非发证数？ 因为葡萄牙 AIMA 审批极慢（积压 55,000+ 件），投资者是先把钱打进基金再申请，但证可能几年后才发。基金净流入才是"新申请量"的真实代理指标。IMGA 2023年报披露实际有 373 位 ARI 投资者（来自 36 个国籍），占基金 AUM 的 ~90%。截至 2024 年底，GV 项目累计吸收约 €73 亿投资（其中基金/资本转移 €8.37 亿），96% 的新申请走基金路线。 Why use net inflow instead of visa issuance? Portugal's AIMA has a backlog of 55,000+ cases. Investors deposit money into funds first, then apply — visas may take years. Fund net inflows are the real proxy for new applications. IMGA's 2023 report disclosed 373 ARI investors from 36 nationalities (~90% of AUM). By end 2024, GV program absorbed ~EUR7.3B total (EUR837M in funds), with 96% of new applications via the fund route.

数据来源与方法论Sources & Methodology

AUM 数据：来源于各基金官网、DECO Proteste、基金年报/半年报资产负债表、APFIPP 报告。
估算净流入：Net Flow = ΔAUM - (AUM_prev x Fund Return)。股票基金使用PSI收益率作为代理，债券基金使用3%年化估算，混合基金按60/40加权。
PSI 影响估算：New Buying Power = 净流入(非总AUM) x PT Equity % x PSI Overlap。4只基金(Golden Income / Atlantic Bond / Horizon / Heed Top)投向为债券或替代资产，葡股配置约0%，对PSI无影响。
重要声明：本报告仅供研究参考，不构成投资建议。估算值存在误差，实际数据以各基金官方披露为准。 AUM Data: Sourced from fund websites, DECO Proteste, annual/semi-annual reports, APFIPP statistics.
Estimated Net Inflow: Net Flow = ΔAUM - (AUM_prev x Fund Return). Equity funds use PSI returns as proxy; bond funds use 3% annualized estimate; mixed funds use 60/40 weighting.
PSI Impact: New Buying Power = Net Inflow (not total AUM) x PT Equity % x PSI Overlap. 4 funds (Golden Income / Atlantic Bond / Horizon / Heed Top) invest in bonds or alternatives with ~0% PT equity exposure.
